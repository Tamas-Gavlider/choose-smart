How to play?

Objective of the Game

Navigate through the story by making choices that affect the outcome.

Game controls

Click on the icon on the bottom to start playing the game.

During the game click on the options presented to make a choice.

Making decisions

Every decision can lead to different paths in the story.

Endings

The game features multiple possible endings based on your decisions. Try different approaches to discover all the endings. Once you reach the final decision, you can start over and explore different paths by clicking on the icon. Look for the button at the bottom of the screen to begin a new journey.

Enhance the experience

For full experience, make sure your sound is on. Tap the icon at the bottom corner to mute or unmute the audio.
