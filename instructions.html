How to play?

Objective of the Game

Navigate through the story by making choices that affect the outcome.

Game controls

Click on the options presented to make a choice.

Making decisions

Every decision can lead to different paths in the story. Some choices may result in immediate consequences, while others might affect the storyline further down the line. Your decisions can lead to different endings, so think carefully before choosing.

Endings

The game features multiple possible endings based on your decisions. Try different approaches to discover all the endings and see how the story changes. Once you reach the final decision, you can start over and explore different paths by clicking on the "Reset Game" button. Look for the button at the bottom of the screen to begin a new journey.

Enhance the experience

For full experience, make sure your sound is on. Tap the sound icon in the corner to mute or unmute the audio.
